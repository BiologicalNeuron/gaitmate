GAIT MATE

Gait Mate is an affordable, real-time sensor insole that lets you track your walking or running style. By using our mobile application, you can visualise your walking or running patterns and identify excessive pressures on your foot. We hope that early detection of common running abnormalities like excessive pronation can help professionals such as personal trainers or podiatrists optimise your function and try to prevent potential injuries
